Privacy Notice

Japan Queue Live V1 Beta does not require an account. The ticket number entered in the browser is processed locally and is not intentionally transmitted or stored by JQL.

Operational data

The service may use standard hosting logs for security, uptime and debugging. Do not enter names, residence-card numbers, application numbers or other personal information.

Third-party source

Live queue information is read from a public source operated separately from JQL. JQL is not a government service.
